## KEY FINDINGS

** 2024 BREEDING EXPLOSION:**
- **FL (Recently Fledged Young)**: 43 observations (MASSIVE SUCCESS!)
- **F (Flyover)**: 42 observations  
- **CF (Carrying Food to Young)**: 35 observations
- **FY (Feeding Young)**: 23 observations
- **N (Nest Building)**: 15 observations
- **CN (Carrying Nesting Material)**: 12 observations

** HISTORICAL COMPARISON:**
- **2005-2013**: Most breeding codes had 1-4 observations per year
- **2014-2023**: Gradual increase in breeding activity
- **2024**: Unprecedented breeding activity with 10-40x more observations per code

** BREEDING SUCCESS INDICATORS:**
2024 shows exceptional reproductive success with high numbers of:
- **Recently fledged young (FL)**: Direct evidence of successful breeding
- **Adults feeding young (FY + CF)**: Active parental care
- **Nest building activity (N + CN)**: Preparation for breeding

** RECOVERY EVIDENCE:**
The 2024 data suggests the Tricolored Blackbird population may be experiencing a significant recovery, with breeding activity at levels far exceeding historical observations in this dataset.

** DATA NOTE:**
2025 shows minimal data (only 1 observation) as it represents a partial year. As of 6/21/2025 (today),
the year is only about halfway over.
